Pet Care Requirements.

  • 01

    Animals are in healthy condition and do not have fleas. We will first check the condition of the dogs and cats when they arrive. The goal is to maintain the safety and health of dogs, cats and other animals.

  • 02

    If after checking there are lice or skin disease, special treatment must be carried out immediately when you arrive. And animals can still be left in a special room for care. So don't mix it with healthy things.

  • 03

    At times they can freely walk around and play with visitors/other animals without being tied up or caged unless there is a request to be caged or in certain situations.

  • 04

    Special equipment that may be needed may be brought such as food, snacks, vitamins, toys and so on.
